Agent Name: Jackal

Jackal is as calculating as he is brilliant. Once he's hacked into the computer system he can help navigate through the toughest of situations.

Agent Name: Komodo

The only thing faster than Komodo's Blasters are his reflexes. This maverick is a quick-fire expert who can fill a Specter with darts before it has launched a single bit of foam.

Agent Name: Shane

Quick both on his feet and in his wit, this combatant is a jack–of–all–trades who can handle almost anything that blasts foam.

Agent Name: Raven

Raven is a threat to the bots from a distance. As a sniping specialist, Raven can blast a Gadfly out of the air before it has even spotted your party.

Agent Name: Tango

Tango can almost always be found with a big Blaster and a short fuse. This trooper specializes in heavy Blasters and is always ready for the fight.
